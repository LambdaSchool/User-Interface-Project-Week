Our Beginnings


S & J architecture was founded by Sirius Black and James Potter in 1979. Combining Sirius' creative eye and James' attention to detail, what started off as a college buisness project soon took off to become one of the greatest firms of all time.

Only S & J have the Magic and Gravitas to not only Dream big, but to wake up and make those Dreams a reality.
