Our employees are at the heart of our mission. S&J is pioneering a new model of higher education in which the school invests in the students, instead of the other way around. Attending S&J is completely free up-front, and students pay back a portion of their income after they find a high-paying job. If, for some reason, a student is unable to find a job with a salary above the set threshold, they are not required to pay anything. The total amount of repayment is also capped at a predetermined maximum amount.
